Origami Rabbit!  Level: Medium

1 / 10
Follow the instructions below to make this Origami rabbit.
2 / 10
Start with a square piece of origami paper with white side up. If you only have regular 8.5x11 paper, follow these instructions to make it into a square sheet.
3 / 10
Fold the paper in half by folding the top corner to the bottom corner, then unfold.
4 / 10
Now fold the left corner to the right corner, then unfold.
5 / 10
It should look like this after the previous step.
6 / 10
Fold a small portion of the triangle base up as shown.
7 / 10
Fold the left and right bottom edges up at the center as shown. These are the origami rabbit ears.
8 / 10
It should look like this after the previous step.
9 / 10
Turn the figure over.
10 / 10
Fold the top corner of the head down as shown.Fold the bottom corner of the chin up as shown, then draw on the face and you're done. Now you know how to make an origami rabbit!
