Origami Cat!  Level: Easy

1 / 8
Follow the steps below to make this Easy Origami Cat.
2 / 8
Start with a square piece of origami paper. If you only have regular 8.5x11 paper, follow these instructions to make a square sheet. You can also use these colorful Printable Origami Paper.
3 / 8
Fold paper in half by folding the bottom corner to top corner.
4 / 8
Fold in half again by folding left corner to right corner.
5 / 8
Unfold.
6 / 8
Form the ears by folding the left and right corners of the triangle up at an angle, like what you see in the picture.
7 / 8
Fold a portion of the top corner down.
8 / 8
Turn the figure over.Draw some eyes and whiskers!
