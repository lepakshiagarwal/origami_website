Origami Dog!  Level: Easy

1 / 8
Follow the steps below to make this Easy Origami Dog.
2 / 8
Start with a square piece of origami paper. If you only have regular 8.5x11 paper, follow these instructions to make a square sheet.
3 / 8
Fold paper in half by folding the top corner to the bottom corner.
4 / 8
Fold the triangle in half by folding the left corner to the right corner.
5 / 8
Unfold./div>
6 / 8
Let's make the ears! Fold both corners of the triangle down at an angle like you see in the picture.
7 / 8
The head's too pointy! Fold the top corner to the back.
8 / 8
Unless you want a pointy chin, fold the bottom corner back also. Give your dog a face by drawing the eyes and nose!
